Wine Cooler Maintenance Programs

Preventive care keeps your wine storage running efficiently and protects your collection.

Preventive maintenance is cheaper than emergency repairs. Annual service keeps wine coolers running efficiently.

Regular maintenance extends wine cooler life, prevents costly repairs, and ensures optimal performance. Like fine wine, wine storage systems need proper care. Annual maintenance programs catch problems early, before they become emergencies.

We offer comprehensive maintenance programs for all premium wine cooler brands. Our annual service includes cleaning, calibration, inspection, and preventive care to keep your wine storage running perfectly.

What's Included in Maintenance

Cleaning & Inspection

Thorough cleaning of condenser coils, evaporator coils, and interior. Visual inspection of all components, door seals, and electrical connections.

Temperature Calibration

Calibrate temperature sensors and control systems. Verify dual-zone operation and temperature accuracy. Ensure proper temperature control.

Compressor Service

Check compressor operation, electrical connections, and refrigerant levels. Identify potential compressor problems before failure.

Door Seal Inspection

Inspect door seals for wear or damage. Replace seals if needed. Ensure proper door seal for energy efficiency.

Humidity System Check

Check humidity control systems, water reservoirs, and condensation management. Ensure proper humidity levels for wine storage.

Electrical System Check

Test electrical components, control boards, and wiring. Identify potential electrical problems before they cause failures.

Benefits of Annual Maintenance

  • Prevent Expensive Repairs — Catch problems early before they become costly failures.
  • Extend Equipment Life — Proper maintenance extends wine cooler life significantly.
  • Maintain Efficiency — Clean coils and calibrated systems run more efficiently, saving energy.
  • Protect Your Collection — Ensure temperature and humidity control protect your wine investment.
  • Peace of Mind — Know your wine storage is running properly and your collection is safe.
  • Priority Service — Maintenance customers get priority scheduling for any repairs needed.

Maintenance Program Options

Annual Maintenance

One-time annual service visit. Includes all maintenance services listed above. Perfect for wine coolers in good condition.

Bi-Annual Maintenance

Two service visits per year. Recommended for older units or heavy-use wine coolers. More frequent care prevents problems.

Maintenance Contracts

Annual contracts with scheduled maintenance visits. Priority service and discounted repair rates. Best value for multiple units or commercial applications.

Call us to discuss which maintenance program is right for your wine cooler. We'll customize a program based on your brand, model, and usage.

When to Schedule Maintenance

Schedule maintenance:

  • Annually for most wine coolers
  • Before peak wine storage seasons
  • After moving or relocating unit
  • If you notice any performance issues
  • For older units (10+ years), consider bi-annual service

Best time: Schedule maintenance during off-peak seasons when your collection is smaller, or before adding new wines.
